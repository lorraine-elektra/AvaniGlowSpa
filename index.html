Spa FAQs

Hybrid and classic mappings average two to three weeks before a lash fill restores fullness. Sleeping on your side, sauna visits, or oil-heavy serums shorten wear, so ask us about a tailored aftercare pouch.
Off-peak afternoons sometimes have same-day lash or wax slots, though weekends book fast. Sending a WhatsApp note with timing preferences beats walking in blindly and keeps your technician reserved.
Arrive exfoliated lightly the day prior, skip retinol on the targeted area forty-eight hours ahead, hydrate well, and wear breathable fabrics afterward. Sensitive skin clients should mention meds so we tweak temperature and balm selection.
Text us dated photos within forty-eight hours. Most legitimate prep lifts are repaired complimentary; habitual picking or acetone-heavy removers outside the studio usually voids gratis fixes but we gladly coach safer removal.
Please give us at least 24 hours notice to cancel or reschedule your appointment. Late cancellations may forfeit any deposit held. Message us on WhatsApp anytime to change your booking — we are happy to help.
We accept Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo) and cash at the spa. If you prefer to pay by card, please confirm card availability when you book so we can be ready for you.
Everyone is welcome at Avani Glow Spa. Children are invited when accompanied by a parent or guardian so we can keep the space calm and safe for all guests.
Yes — gift vouchers are available for any service or amount. Contact us on WhatsApp to arrange a voucher for someone special.
